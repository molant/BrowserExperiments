Flexible Box Model

The (W3C) Flexible Box Model gives us better control over the presentation of block elements. In the experiment the blue (Box 2.*) are allowed to take up twice the spare space as their neighbours Box 1 & 3. And all box elements are reversed. The flexie shim by doctyper brings support to all the IE < 10 browsers and Opera 11.
