CSS3 Animation

The (w3c) CSS3 Animations define a way to add animation to page elements when a style rule is applied. On modern devices, such as the iPhone and iPad, the hardware-enhanced CSS3 Animations and Transitions are more performant than the old method of making timed style changes.


This approach replaces what has for a long time been possible with javascript. A potential shim would be to use the popular jQuery.animate.
